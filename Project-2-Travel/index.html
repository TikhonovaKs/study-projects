What didn't we see there?

According to VTsIOM polls, 95% of Russians dream of going somewhere, but only 36% plan to spend their holidays in their native country. Like, what have we not seen here at home? In fact, Russia is a whole universe with the gentle sea of the south, the dense forests of the Sayans and the harsh ice of the Putorana plateau. And you can also see all these beauties without millions in your account, a passport and many hours of flights. Like, for example, Vera Bashmakova, a brave young mother who took three children in her arms, put them in her Lada and drove 20 thousand kilometers in his native country. We have selected and described some interesting places worthy of your vacation.

Curonian spit

URL

park-kosa.ru

Here, in the middle of forests and sand dunes, you can see two water horizons - the calm Curonian bay on one side and the rippling Baltic Sea on the other. A unique natural area on the edge of the Russian enclave.

The Kaliningrad region does not end there. For the traveler and explorer in the same place next door - the westernmost point of Russia, the Baltic Spit - and the German heritage of a scattering of small seaside towns. The atmosphere of these places eliminates the fuss, dipping into the tranquility of nature and the smell of steel, cool sea.

Kola peninsula

URL

National Geographic

Almost the entire peninsula is located beyond the Arctic Circle. Sami tundra, from which to the south - taiga, and on north - the Arctic Ocean, pretending to be the Barents Sea.

Perhaps you watched Zvyagintsev and even heard the story of the Arctic festival in Teriberka. Perhaps the word "Khibiny" did not remain under the snow of school memories of geography lessons. Perhaps you were not interested penetrating the earth's crust with an ultra-deep well, and from apatites you have long been covered with apathy. But your dream is to see the northern lights begin to come true with a ticket to Murmansk.

Altai

URL

Facebook

Altai is one of the most beautiful places in Russia. First of all, because of the mountains: if you go along the ridge, you will see slopes strewn with pine trees, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open the windows in the car, you can get acquainted with invisible miracle of these places - mountain air.

The climate in Altai is temperate, so it is best to go here in summer. So you will see all the variety local flora and fauna. Moose roam the forests of Altai, eagles fly over the ridges, and roe deer graze on the plains. And famous manuls are also inhabitants of the Altai Territory.

Winter Baikal

URL

https://vk.com/baikalmile

Everyone knows Baikal as the largest lake in the world. Many also know that this is the biggest source fresh water and one of the most beautiful places in Russia.

Of course, it's all true. But Baikal is still an ideal place for skiing competitions. It's that kind sport, when a skier ties himself to a motorcycle, and the tandem tries to develop as much speed as possible on the ice. March 2019 year at the festival "Baikal Mile" a world record was set - 197.011 km / h.

Karelia

URL

http://vodlozero.ru/

Siberia ends not in the Urals, but in Karelia: the Siberian larch that forms the taiga does not grow west of Vodlozero. But here it towers up to 30 meters - the forests of Karelian national parks due to impenetrable swamps never knew the axe. Some pines are more than half a millennium old. Touch a living being that has seen the sun before Ivan the Terrible saw him. In the virgin forest for a hundred kilometers you will not find a path. And on rare paths trees a couple of meters from the ground are marked with bear claws. Let everyone know who's boss.
